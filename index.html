how it works

Simple enough to
start today.

01
Connect your wallet
Any EVM wallet works — MetaMask, Rabby, anything. No new setup required.
02
Set your conditions
Choose your check-in frequency and how long before inactivity triggers your vault. You stay in full control.
03
Write your messages
Lock messages for the people you love. Letters, wallet notes, words you never said — encrypted onchain until needed.
04
Check in daily
One signature a day keeps the signal alive. Build your streak. Watch your Future Self Insight unlock on Day 4.
05
Your signal does the rest
If you go dark, LastSignal monitors, alerts, and ultimately protects the people you designated. Fully onchain. No middleman.
